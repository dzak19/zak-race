Rules of the Race
  • All clues can be accesesed on the My Race tab and should be completed sequentially.
  • Clues will be time released, but we are dealing with different time zones, so on the honor system we're asking that you not open clues unless you have completed the previous one.
  • This is a beta test and mistakes are bound to happen. Please adjust and create an alternate leg of the race when this happens and log it in your journal.
  • Each team will have four total days of racing - three full days and two half days.
  • Every day exactly 10 pictures should be submitted of completed challenges. There will also be optional bonus challenges for extra points. The more beautiful, dramatic, or funny the pictures are, the more points you can get. Always try to be in the picture yourself when possible.
  • A list of challenges accomplished, start time, finish time, break time, and photos should be emailed to elizabethp.karis@gmail.com daily.
  • Please keep a log of things we did right and ways we can improve to be discussed over Shabbat. Be kind but do not hold back.
